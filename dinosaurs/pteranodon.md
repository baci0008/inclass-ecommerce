*Pteranodon* is an amazing flying prehistoric creature!

The pteranodon lived during the [Late Cretaceous Period](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Late_Cretaceous). 

##Features

- Toothless
- Not dinosaour
- Average adult male was 5.6 metres
